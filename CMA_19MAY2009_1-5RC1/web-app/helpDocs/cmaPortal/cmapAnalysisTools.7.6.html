Changing the PCA Display
To display PC1 versus PC2, PC1 versus PC3, or PC2 versus PC3, click the appropriate tab above the plot.
PC1 is the first principal component, which accounts for most of the variation in the multidimensional data set. After correcting for the variation due to PC1, PC2—the second principal component—is the dimension that accounts for most of the remaining variation in the dataset. Subsequent analyses correct for remaining variation.
