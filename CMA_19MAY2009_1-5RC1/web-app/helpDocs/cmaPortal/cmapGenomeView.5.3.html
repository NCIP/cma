Launching and Navigating the Heatmap Viewer
The Heatmap Viewer launches automatically whenever you click one of links in the Copy Number, Gene Expression Data, or Methylation Data sections. You can also access the Heatmap Viewer directly.
How to Access the Heatmap Viewer
•
At the top of the Genome View page, click the Easy access to genome heatmaps link.
Controls in the Heatmap Viewer allow you to zoom in to any location, determine the heatmap’s color and contrast, and to research your data further through links to the Cancer Genome Workbench.
To access the Heatmap Viewer documentation, click the Help button at the top of the Heatmap Viewer, and select Documentation.
