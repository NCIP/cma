About the Cancer Molecular Analysis Portal
The CMA Portal provides access to query and analysis tools; and to data in The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA). TCGA is designed to allow the integration of clinical, genomic characterization, and large-scale genomic sequencing data of all samples. TCGA researchers characterize and sequence the genomes of up to 500 samples of both tumor and matched normal tissues.
For more information about the available data, refer to The Cancer Genome Atlas’ (TCGA’s) website at:
http://cancergenome.nih.gov/components/dmbca.asp
