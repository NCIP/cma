Deleting Lists
You can delete any list on the List Management page, but you can not retrieve a list that you have deleted.
How to How to Delete a List
1.
At the top of the List Management page, click the type of lists you want to delete (PatientDID Lists, Gene Lists, or Reporter Lists).
You can not retrieve a list once you have deleted it.
2.
Click the delete icon (x) next to the name of the list you want to delete.
The list is deleted.
