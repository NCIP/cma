Logging In From the Home Page
If you have a CMA Portal account, you can log in from the Home page before you use any of the CMA Portal tools.
How to Log In to the CMA Portal
1.
In the Existing Users side bar, type your username and password in the text boxes provided.
2.
Click Log In.
