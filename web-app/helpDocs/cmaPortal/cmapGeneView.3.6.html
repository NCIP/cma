Log2 Intensity Gene Expression Plot Details
The Log2 Intensity Gene Expression Plot displays average expression intensities for the gene of interest based on Affymetrix GeneChip arrays (U133A arrays). Multiple probe sets (for some genes) are designed to measure the expression of the gene of interest. For more information on the probe set design strategy for human genes, see the Affymetrix website at http://www.affymetrix.com.
