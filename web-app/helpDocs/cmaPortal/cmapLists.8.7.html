Viewing and Managing Lists
The following figure illustrates the PatientDID, Gene, and Reporter lists as they appear with their respective content when managing lists. Default CMA Portal lists are displayed in black text; custom lists you may have created in your current session are displayed in red text. In addition to the lists displayed in the figure, registered CMA Portal users can create custom clinical data lists. See Creating Custom Lists.
Note:
You may have to scroll down the page to see all lists.
The CMA Portal enables you to manage the content of predefined and custom lists as follows:
•
View details of a list’s content. See Viewing List Details
•
View a list’s creation date and notes. See Viewing List Creation Data and Notes
•
Delete a list. See Deleting Lists
•
Delete an item from a list. See Deleting Items From a List
