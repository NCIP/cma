Creating Custom Lists
You can define custom lists to facilitate your analysis in the following ways:
•
Combine two or more lists. See Creating New Lists From Existing Lists
•
Upload your own lists automatically. See Creating New Lists From Your Files
•
Type lists manually. See Creating New Lists Manually
