Generating Pathway Diagrams
Pathway diagrams enable you to investigate genetic anomalies at a cellular level.
How to Generate a Pathway Diagram
1.
Do one of the following to navigate to the Gene View workspace:
•
From the Home page, click Gene View.
- or -
•
From any page in the portal, click the Gene View tab.
The Gene View workspace appears.
2.
Under Pathway Visualization, click the View Pathway Visualization link, select.
The Pathway Visualization and Pathway Gene Anomalies page appears in a new browser.
If the pathway diagram is not displayed, install the SVG plugin on your computer. For more information about installing the SVG plugin, see Installing the SVG Plugin.
The Pathway Visualization and Pathway Gene Anomalies page is divided into 2 sections as follows:
•
An interactive diagram of the pathway of interest appears at the top of the page. The basis for the visualization is a BioCarta pathway diagram. The selected pathway is depicted as if projected onto a morphological illustration of a cell. It depicts gene-to-gene and other molecular interactions in a graphical interface.
•
A Pathway Genes Anomalies table appears at the bottom of the page. It lists the genes for the selected pathway and the anomalies associated with each of them.
Click the Help icon at the top of the page to access online help for the Pathway Viewer.
