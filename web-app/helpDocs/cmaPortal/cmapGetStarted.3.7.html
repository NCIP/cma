Requesting a Username and Password
If you are a first-time CMA Portal user and want to access controlled TCGA datasets, click the Register link.
How to Register For a Username and Password
1.
In the Additional Information section of the side bar, click Register.
The New Users page appears.
2.
In the Name area, type your first and last names in the text boxes provided.
3.
In the Contact Information area, type your email address and other contact information in the text boxes provided.
The system will send your account information to this email address.
4.
In the Verification area, type the series of letters and numbers in the text box exactly as displayed in the verification image.
5.
Click Register, or, to clear all text boxes, click Reset.
All registration information requested is required.
6.
If you clicked Register, the system displays a message indicating that your account information will be sent to you by email.
7.
When you receive your account information, follow the instructions in the email message to apply for a Data User Certificate (DUC). For further information about data access and DUCs, see http://cancergenome.nih.gov/dataportal/data/access/closed/duc/.
