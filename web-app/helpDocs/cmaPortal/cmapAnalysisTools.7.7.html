Selecting and Saving Samples in PCA Plots
The Samples area enables you to select, review, and save samples in the plot.
How to How to Select and Save Samples
1.
On the PCA plot, drag your cursor over a sample of interest, or to select a group of samples, drag your cursor across two or more samples. A red outline appears around your sample(s), and the sample name(s) is/are added to the Samples list to the right of the plot.
Hover your cursor over a sample name to view the entire ID.
2.
To save the sample list, in the Save Samples field, type a unique name for the list, and then click Save Samples.
The sample list appears on the List Management page. See Viewing and Managing Lists
