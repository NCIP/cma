Selecting Samples in PCA Plots
The Samples area enables you to select, review, and save samples in the plot.
To select individual samples, drag your cursor over the sample of interest. A red outline appears around your sample, and the sample name is added to the Samples list to the right of the plot.
Hover your cursor over a sample name to view the entire ID.
To select a sample group, drag your cursor across two or more samples.
