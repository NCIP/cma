Logging In From Workspace Pages
If you do not have a CMA Portal account, you can use any of the CMA Portal tools with open-access data. Afterward, you may decide to register for access to controlled data.
How to Log In To the CMA Portal From Workspace Pages
1.
Click any of the views or tools on the menu bar, for example, Analysis Tools.
2.
Read and accept the provisions.
In this example, the Analysis Tools workspace appears.
3.
Click the Login/Register tab.
The Login page appears.
4.
Type your username and password in the text boxes provided, and then click Submit.
5.
If you do not have an account, click Register to register for a username and password, and then follow the instructions in Requesting a Username and Password.
