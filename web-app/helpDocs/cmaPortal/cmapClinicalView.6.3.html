Searching for TCGA Clinical Data
Open-access data groups are available to all CMA Portal users. For instructions on searching for TCGA open-access data, see Searching for TCGA Clinical Data. Other search criteria are available to registered viewers only. This serves to protect patient privacy. For instructions on searching for TCGA controlled-access data, see Searching for TCGA Clinical Data.
For instructions on creating Kaplan-Meier plots based on your sample selections, see Creating Sample-Based KM Plots.
