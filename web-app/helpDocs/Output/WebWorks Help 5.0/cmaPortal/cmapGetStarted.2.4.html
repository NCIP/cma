Logging In to the Cancer Molecular Analysis Portal
If you intend to conduct your research using open-access data only, you do not need to log in to the CMA Portal. However, to take advantage of controlled-access data, you must provide a username and password. See Requesting a Username and Password
All REMBRANDT and TARGET data is open-access.
How to Log In to the CMA Portal
1.
In the Existing Users side bar on the Home page, type your username and password in the text boxes provided.
2.
Click Log In.
A message confirms that you have logged in successfully.
The system logs you out automatically after ten minutes of inactivity. Because the CMA Portal does not save your custom lists from one session to the next, you will not be able to retrieve your lists after you have been logged out.
