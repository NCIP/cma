Logging Out
To log out of Cancer Molecular Analysis Portal, in the Cancer Molecular Analysis Portal workspace, click the LOGOUT tab in the upper right-hand corner.
You are logged out of Cancer Molecular Analysis Portal and returned to the Home page.
The system logs you out automatically after 30 minutes of inactivity. Because the CMA Portal does not save your custom lists from one session to the next, you will not be able to retrieve your lists after you have been logged out.
