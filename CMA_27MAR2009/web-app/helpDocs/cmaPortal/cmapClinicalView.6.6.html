Searching for REMBRANDT Clinical Data
Because all patient-related data in REMBRANDT is open-access, clinical views are more restricted than their controlled-access counterparts.
How to Search for REMBRANDT Patient Data in the Clinical View Workspace
1.
Do one of the following to navigate to the Clinical View workspace:
•
From the Home page, click Clinical View.
- or -
•
From any workspace in the portal, click the Clinical View tab.
The Clinical View workspace appears.
2.
Select the groups to query from the Sample Groups list by doing any of the following:
•
To select a single group, click the group name.
•
To select several contiguous groups, click the first group, press and hold the Shift key, and select the last group in the series. Or, drag your cursor from the first group to the last group in the series.
•
To select several discontinuous groups, click one group and Ctrl + click additional groups.
Note:
Steps 3 through 5 below are optional
3.
To filter the query further, select criteria and values from the drop-down lists provided. The table below lists the available criteria and values.
Criterion Name
Instructions and Values
Restrict by Age
Select the Restrict by Age check box, and then specify the lower and upper age limits for the patient’s age at diagnosis.
Gender
Specify the patient’s gender:
M (male)
F (female)
O (other) – Indicates that metadata for gender does not exist or has not been recorded.
Restrict by Survival
Select the Restrict by Survival check box, and then specify the lower and upper limits of the number of months the patient survived from the date of diagnosis
Disease
Specify the disease:
•
All Glioma
•
Astrocytoma
•
Cell Line
•
GBM
•
Mixed
•
Non-tumorous
•
Oligodendroglioma
•
Unknown
Race
Specify the North American Association of Central Cancer Registries (NAACRR) values for race:
•
White
•
Black
•
Native Hawaiian
•
Asian NOS
•
Other
•
Unknown
4.
In the Query Name field, type a unique name for the query.
To prevent loss of data, do not type a name that you used for a previous query in your current session. The CMA Portal stores queries for the duration of your current CMA Portal session only.
5.
To clear the values entered on the page and enter new values, click Clear.
6.
To submit the query, click Submit.
The CMA Portal processes your query and displays results in a new browser window.
For information about the results, see Viewing Clinical Reports. For instructions on creating Kaplan-Meier plots based on your sample selections, see Creating Sample-Based KM Plots.
