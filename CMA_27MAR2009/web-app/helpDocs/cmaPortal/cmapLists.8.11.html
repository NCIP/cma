Deleting Items From a List
You can delete any item on the List Management page, but you can not retrieve an item that you have deleted.
How to How to Delete One or More List Items
1.
Click the Details icon next to a list to display its content.
You can not retrieve a list once you have deleted it.
2.
To the right of the item you want to delete, click [delete].
The item is deleted from the list.
